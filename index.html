intro

Kamotho Muriithi is a patriotic Kenyan Youth born and raised in Marmanet Ward, Laikipia County

I am a believer in social democracy, good governance and the rule of law. For the 2022 race I am seeking the elective position as a Member of County Assembly representative, Marmanet Ward.

I hold a BA in Political Science and Public Administration from Karatina University. As an intellect, I have and does not mind challenging and changing the status quo.
The youth make up to 78.3% of the Kenyan population. As such, the ability to bring change in our Kenyan political space, governance and entrenched disparities rests solely in us. Come 2022, I urge all of us to make our vote count in the best way ever.

Agenda

Access to clean water.

Sinking at least one borehole in a radius of 10 km. Rehabilitation of existing dams and dredging more.

Health care.

Ensure that hospitals are fully equipped and that nobody goes home to buy medicine. Pushing for more staff to attend to patients. Registering more people in the NHIF program.

Bursaries.

Transparency in the award of bursaries taking into account the status of all applicants. (Reviewing the current skewed formula followed while awarding bursaries)

Upgrading roads.

Ensure that majority of roads are converted to all weather roads by construction of culverts and bridges.

Empowerment.

Proposing bills to the county assembly that encourage businesses ideas and guaranteed support to such ventures by the county government including quarterly payments of business licenses.
